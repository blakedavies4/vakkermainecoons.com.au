Registered Pedigree Breeder

In Australia, Breeder Registration with a pedigree association is essential to breed and sell pedigree puppies and kittens. We are registered breeders with the Queensland Feline Association (QFA).

When buying from a registered pedigree breeder you have confidence knowing you will receive a well socialised pet that is true to breed standard, has been fed high quality food, kept healthy, and raised in a safe and ethical way.

Purebred in not the same as Pedigree. Kittens with a Pedigree can show a proven family tree of their ancestors whereas purebred kittens do not have any legitimate proof of parentage or if they are even a certain breed.

Home Raised

Our adults and babies are raised in a loving home environment. We do not raise our kittens in outside or out-of-the-way enclosures. This allows us to nurture healthy, secure, outgoing, and affectionate kittens with very impressive temperaments.

They spend their first 3 - 4 weeks living in our bedroom and are then raised with humans, cats and dogs within our living areas and have free access to safe outside areas where they can see birds, wallabies, bandicoots, sometimes deer and other creatures within our property.

Breeding Since 1994

We are small, home-based breeders located near Woodford SE Qld. Our breeding experience started in 1994 specialising in breeding only pedigree Siberian Husky puppies. In 2010 we were introduced to the wonderful world of Maine Coons and have been raising these beautiful babies in our home ever since.

In addition to experience in breeding and raising kittens and puppies, we also have experience in raising many different animals including fish, guinea pigs, love birds and cockatiels. We also have an ex racing/dressage STB, a Clydesdale x STB and a Quarter horse.
